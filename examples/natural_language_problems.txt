A store is having a 25% off sale on all items. If a jacket originally costs $80, how much will it cost after the discount?

Solution: To find the discounted price, first calculate 25% of $80, which is $20. Then subtract that from the original price: $80 - $20 = $60. The jacket will cost $60 after the discount.
